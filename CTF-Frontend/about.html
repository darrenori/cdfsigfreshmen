What is Scavenger Hunt?
A Scavenger Hunt is a special kind of cybersecurity competition designed to challenge its participants to solve computer security problems. The game consists of a series of puzzles where participants must reverse engineer, break, hack, decrypt, or do whatever it takes to solve the challenge. The challenges are all set up with the intent of being hacked, making it an excellent, legal way to get hands-on experience.
About TP CDFSIG
TP CDFSIG is a student interest group representing the Cybersecurity and Digital Forensics Cohort.
Our aim is to make every CDF student's life here slightly better through our activties as well as hone their cyber knowledge from events or courses we host.
